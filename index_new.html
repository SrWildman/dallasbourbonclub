Dallas Bourbon Club

ABOUT US

We love bourbon

Started in 2013, Dallas Bourbon Club, has grown into the premier society for bourbon enthusiasts in North Texas. Our members come from diverse backgrounds with various careers, but all hold a common interest in bourbon.

The DBC holds quarterly events for members in addition to regular tastings and an annual field trip. If you have a passion for bourbon, drop us a line and see what we are up to. Cheers!

Mission Statement


Networking around Dallas. An appreciation of America's spirit, bourbon. Giving back to our community.
